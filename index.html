Jennifer Zamudio, MS, LPC-Associate | Supervised by Charlsa Placker, LPC-S
Are you feeling helpless, alone, and isolated questioning your purpose and meaning in life?

Are you questioning yourself for decisions that may impact your life path?

Do you sometimes feel like you're getting pulled to many directions and your thoughts are all over the place?

Sometimes life can be overwhelming because we may experience hardships, stressors, life transitions and choices that impact our present. As your therapist, I am here to help you find your inner strengths, equipe you with counseling skills and coping skills to begin the healing process. Together we will recuperate your sense of self for you to find that balance in relationships, career, work and your unique life transition.

WELCOME TO JENNIZ COUNSELING CENTER!
JenniZ Counseling is a safe place where we provide a supportive environment where you can freely explore your current initial concern for therapy. We will work together to create important and meaningful goals during therapy and move away from irrational thoughts, feelings or behaviors. My mission is to serve, help and empower you to remember your self-worth and gain the necessary tools to achieve your fullest potential to create mental health & wellness.
